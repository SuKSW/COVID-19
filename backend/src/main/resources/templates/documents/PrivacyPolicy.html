Purpose of the App

The main purpose of the MyHealth Sri Lanka app is to:

  • Help the country to minimize the spread of the COVID-19 disease.
  • Help the general public get verified information on the current COVID-19 outbreak at regular intervals.
  • Provide a way to track and notify if you have been exposed to a person/ persons infected by the COVID-19 virus based on your travel history for the last 14 days.
  • Provide a mechanism to register your information in the disease surveillance system managed by the Ministry of Health Sri Lanka.

Privacy:

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

  • All your location information will be stored and processed locally (in-device).
  • We do not send PII (registration details) and location details to the disease surveillance system of the Ministry of Health unless you explicitly choose to share this information.
  • All news and alerts received by this app are verified by the Ministry of Health.

Information Collection and Use:

The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The location information will only be stored locally (in-device) and will not be shared with the central server or the disease surveillance system unless you choose to share when needed. The location trail of an individual who was diagnosed with the COVID-19 virus is processed locally and you will receive relevant feedback based on the matching process. If there is a possible contact history based on the location details, the app will prompt you to register with the disease surveillance system.

Further, a third-party product named Log Data is used to collect information in case of an error on the app. Log Data may include information, such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device model, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

The following third-party services are used by this app to collect information that identifies you:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Log Data

Cookies

This Service does not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services.
